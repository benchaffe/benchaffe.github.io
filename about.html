About Me

Short Bio

I am a graduate from the University of Surrey where I studied a BSc in Economics and Mathematics. I originally started coding to make mods for games such as minecraft. Throughout my time at university I developed a deeper interest in data analysis through both economics and mathematics modules that I studied.

While studying at university I also worked full time as a Team Leader for Stonegate Pub Company at Popworld Guildford. While working here I completed Alberts Award for Team Leaders and was involved in the Pubwatch Welfare Champions scheme which led to me becoming first aid trained. I also gained a BIIAB Level 2 Award for Personal Licence Holders and a Personal Alcohol Licence from Guildford Borough Council.
